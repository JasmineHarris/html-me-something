What are some ways I can be physically fit at the office?

On average, adults need at least 2.5 hours of physical activity per week to stay fit along with strength training on at least two of those days.

While at the office, a fit geek can do the following to promote bloodflow:

  • Take active breaks and lunches
  • Be an active commuter
  • Open a desktop gym...if your boss is cool with that
  • Start an in office exercise team
